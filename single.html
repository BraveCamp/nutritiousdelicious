    Chicken Pepper Pasta

    ×

    Ingredients

    • 1 cup rotini pasta, uncooked
    • 1/2 lb. boneless skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-size pieces
    • 1/2 cup thin green pepper strips
    • 1/4 cup chopped onions
    • 1 cup pasta sauce
    • 1/4 cup shredded mozzarella cheese

    Directions

    1. Cook pasta as directed on package, omitting salt.
    2. Meanwhile, cook chicken in large skillet sprayed with cooking spray on medium heat 5 min., stirring occasionally. Add vegetables; cook 3 to 5 min. or until chicken is done and vegetables are crisp-tender, stirring occasionally.
    3. Drain pasta. Add to chicken mixture along with the pasta sauce; mix lightly. Cook 5 min. or until heated through, stirring occasionally. Top with cheese; cover. Remove from heat. Let stand 2 to 3 min. or until cheese is melted.
